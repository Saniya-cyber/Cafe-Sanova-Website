Need Help?

We’re here to make your Café Sanova experience smooth and enjoyable. Find answers to common questions below.

Q1: How can I make a reservation?

You can reserve a table by calling us at +91 9322799275 or sending us a message via our Contact page.

Q2: Do you offer take-away or delivery?

Yes! All our menu items are available for take-away. Delivery is available through local delivery apps.

Q3: What are your opening hours?

We’re open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Q4: Can I provide feedback?

Absolutely! Visit our Contact page and send us a message. We love hearing from our customers.

Q5: Do you cater for events or parties?

Yes, we offer catering for small events. Please contact us for details.

Still need help? Send us a message and we’ll get back to you promptly.
